About SmartAttend

Reimagining attendance for the modern classroom.

Our Mission

Roll calls waste 10 minutes of every class. Paper registers get lost. Spreadsheets get messy. SmartAttend replaces all of that with a clean, private, browser-based attendance tracker that anyone can use in minutes.

Who it's for

  • School and college teachers managing daily attendance
  • Coaching centers and tuition classes
  • Workshops, training programs, and bootcamps
  • Sports teams, clubs, and community organizations

The SmartAttend Difference

Unlike heavy attendance software, SmartAttend runs entirely in your browser. There's no signup, no cloud, no monthly fee. Your student data is stored locally on your device — and you can export it as CSV whenever you need it.
